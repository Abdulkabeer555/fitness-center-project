Nutrition Tips

High Protein Diet

Protein helps build and repair muscle. Include chicken, eggs, tofu, and legumes in your meals.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily. Hydration is key for energy and recovery.

Balanced Meals

Combine carbs, proteins, and healthy fats in your diet to keep your body fueled throughout the day.

Pre-Workout Meal

Eat slow-digesting carbs like oats or bananas 30–60 minutes before workout for energy boost.

Post-Workout Nutrition

After a workout, refuel with protein and carbs to help muscle recovery. Try whey protein with fruits.

Supplements

Use supplements like whey, BCAA, or multivitamins only when needed — always consult a trainer first.
